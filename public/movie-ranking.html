David's Greatest Movies

3 Movies I Resonated With

The Boy and the Beast

The film speaks to me as a child who didn't find joy and fondness in normal children activities,
and I also didn't have my parents around and sought parenthood from strangers.
I developed my martial arts from a stranger as well and he grew to be like a father to me.

Howl's Moving Castle

The film speaks of love and shows how to love people for their
partners and accepting them for who they are. In many ways it shows that
love can flourish even through time.

American History X

The movie speaks of the cycle of hatred and how it will only continue to feed itself
as long as we give into our rage. The film does depict a racist, but he is someone
who has taken the time to self reflect upon his character. Not for himself but for his brother.
